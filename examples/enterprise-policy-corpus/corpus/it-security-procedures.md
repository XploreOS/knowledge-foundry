## 1. VPN access for contractors

To request VPN access for a new contractor:

1. The sponsoring manager opens a **Contractor Access** ticket in the IT
   Service Desk portal (category: Network > VPN).
2. Attach the signed contractor non-disclosure agreement and the statement
   of work end date.
3. Information Security reviews within two business days; access is
   provisioned with an expiry matching the SOW end date, capped at 180 days.
4. Renewals require a fresh ticket — expiries are never extended in place.

*Topic tags: it_and_security, onboarding.*

## 2. Reporting a lost or stolen company laptop

Report immediately, at any hour:

1. Call the Security Operations hotline (ext. 5911) or use the **Report
   Device Loss** button in the IT portal.
2. SecOps remotely locks the device and rotates the user's credentials.
3. File the follow-up form within 24 hours for asset-recovery and, where
   personal data may be involved, privacy-team assessment.

Delayed reporting (over 24 hours) is a policy violation reviewed by the
employee's director.

*Topic tags: it_and_security.*

## 4. Security-awareness training

All staff and contractors complete security-awareness training in the first
three days of access and annually thereafter. Non-completion suspends
network access automatically.
